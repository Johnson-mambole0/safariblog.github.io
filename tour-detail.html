Overview

The classic Tanzania circuit

This five-day safari is the perfect introduction to Tanzania's northern circuit — the most celebrated wildlife region in Africa. You'll spend two full days in the Serengeti following lion prides, cheetah families and vast elephant herds across the golden plains.

A full day inside the extraordinary Ngorongoro Crater follows — a natural enclosure that traps an incredible concentration of animals including the rare black rhino. Your final game drive is at Lake Manyara, famous for its tree-climbing lions and million-strong flamingo flocks.

All transport is in a private custom Land Cruiser with pop-up roof. You'll camp or stay in lodges — we'll match accommodation to your style and budget.

Day-by-Day Itinerary
Your guide will collect you from Kilimanjaro Airport (JRO) or your Arusha hotel and transfer you to your overnight accommodation. In the evening, sit down with your guide for a full safari briefing — parks to visit, what to expect, packing advice and any questions you have. Overnight in Arusha.
After an early breakfast, depart for the Serengeti (approx. 5–6 hours including game viewing en route through Ngorongoro Conservation Area). Arrive in time for an afternoon game drive through the Central Serengeti — lion prides are often visible from the road by late afternoon. Sundowner drinks on the plains. Dinner and overnight at your Serengeti camp.
A full day entirely dedicated to the Serengeti. Start with an early morning game drive when predators are most active — cheetahs hunting, lions at kills, and leopards descending from their trees. Return to camp for a midday rest and lunch, then head out again in the afternoon for golden-hour game viewing. Optional: hot air balloon safari at dawn (additional cost).
Depart early and drive to the Ngorongoro Crater rim (2,200m altitude). Descend into the crater floor for a full day game drive — the 304 km² enclosed ecosystem means extraordinary wildlife density. Search for black rhino, vast hippo pools, lion prides, spotted hyena dens and the flamingo-pink shores of Lake Magadi. Picnic lunch at the designated hippo pool viewpoint. Ascend the crater and overnight at rim lodge with views across the caldera.
Morning game drive in Lake Manyara National Park — a compact but diverse park with forest trails, tree-climbing lions and the vast soda lake alive with flamingos, pelicans and storks. Picnic lunch. Early afternoon transfer to Arusha for flights home, or continue to your Zanzibar extension. Our team will ensure you make your departure comfortably.

Key items: lightweight binoculars, quality sunscreen, insect repellent, a sun hat, comfortable walking shoes, and a good camera with extra batteries.
